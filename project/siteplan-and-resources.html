Website Planning Document

1. Site Name and Logo:

Planttitas.net

“Planttitas” evoke taking care of fragile plants. It is cute, welcoming, and goes along with beginner-friendly advice and guides on gardening concepts. The name is bilingual-friendly, brand-like, and unique. Plus, the .net extension still feels professional and trustworthy. The logo represents a seed full of potential, being nurtured and brought to life, which aligns beautifully with Planttitas's purpose: Helping plant lovers learn, start small, and watch their gardens grow.

2. Site Purpose:

To inform and engage residents that love gardening. Provides a collaborative space, valuable resources, and a personalized area for users to track their own gardening.
